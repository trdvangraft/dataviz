The Great Recession in the Netherlands

Can you spot the effect of the Great Recession on the Netherlands?

The Great Recession was the most severe economic recession in the United States since the Great Depression of the 1930s (COVID-19 excluded). It affected the entire world: the Netherlands included. Did the recession cause people to be less romantic?

National GDP Growth on Household in the Netherlands

A crisis can also affect marital status.

It is hypothesized that during a crisis, more divorces occur due to the fact that tensions arise from the financial household situation. When things are good and the economy is booming, people tend to marry more. The opposite is also true: are weddings costly in the Netherlands?
