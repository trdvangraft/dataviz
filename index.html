Economic Crises and Mental Health

Economic crises can really affect mental health.

Many studies have hypohtesized the effect of an economic crisis on mental health. We are currently amidst an economic crisis caused by the spread of the COVID-19 virus. It has definitely taken a toll on the mental health of people all around the world. We will look at the case of the Netherlands and explore the hidden dark side that economic crises have had on the population, province by province.

Going through an economic crisis is not easy, in fact many a man has suffered through it. This suffering can spread and manifest on many ways. We will start by looking at what an economic crisis means in terms of GDP decline and how it has affected different provinces in the Netherlands through the years. Later, we will look at how each province has coped with said crises in terms of suicide rates and divorce rates.
